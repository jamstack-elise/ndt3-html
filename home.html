Letter of Intent

   My name is Elisejane and I go by Elise for short. I hold a strong interest in the Web Content Strategist role with your team. After some research, I found ND Creative's GitHub repo, ndt3-html. From there, I learned about the Notre Dame Website Template and thought, "Oh, neat! Let's learn something worth mentioning!" Hence, I set forth and produced some work using HTML/CSS/JavaScript to show my proficiency in building website essentials using the readily available resources. Try, for instance, to click on the 'Welcome!' button above. Or, the Interactive Quality Assurance Checklist on the Sticky Navigation.

I invite you to review what I happily created. After using this theme in application, I've become familiar with a couple of existing modules. Namely, the News and Events modules. I hope to continue learning more about Conductor!

The brand colors were taken from the University of Notre Dame's University Branding webpage, as I've learned ND Creative's award-winning team are custodians to the University's brand. Go Irish!☘️

Sincerely,

Elisejane C. Plecnik
